Roasted Broccoli

This Roasted Broccoli recipe is super easy, healthy, and delicious!

If you have an Air Fryer it will turn out even more crispy, but a regular oven will work fine as well

Ingredients

  • 3 Broccoli crowns, stems trimmed, and cut into equal size florets
  • 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Cayenne Pepper

Instructions

  1. Toss broccoli florets in olive oil until evenly coated
  2. Prehead oven or air fryer to 190 degrees celsius
  3. Season liberally with salt, pepper, garlic pepper, and cayenne pepper
  4. Place in oven or air fryer for 5 minutes
  5. Remove and toss to promote even cooking
  6. Cook for another 4-5 minutes or until desired level of crsipiness is reached
