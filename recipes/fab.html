Fettucine al Burro

This super simple pasta recipe can be seen as a more traditional verison of Fettucine Alfredo, or a zhuzhed up buttered noodles!

Either way it is beyond simple and only requres a few key ingredients

Ingredients

  • 340g Fettucine, dry works but fresh is ideal
  • 6 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 340g fresh grated parmigiano regiano
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Fill a medium pot with water, salt generoulsy, and heat over medium high
  2. When water reaches a rolling boil immerse pasta, agitating immediately to prevent sticking
  3. Cook until al dente and toothsome
  4. Reserve 1 cup of pasta cooking water
  5. Drain Pasta and remove from heat
  6. Add butter and 3/4 of parmigiano reggiano, tossing immediately until well combined
  7. Add pasta cooking water little by little as you mix the pasta, butter, and cheese, until desired sauce consistency is reached.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
