The Bear S2 Boursin Potato Chip Omelette

This omelette shown in season 2 of Hulu's TV show "The Bear" is a twist on the classic French Omelette

By whisking the eggs through a strainer, piping a log of Boursin cheese into the filling of the omelette, and crushing some chips over top, the classic dish is updated with a little modern flair and richness

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons salted butter
  • Boursin Cheese
  • Fresh Chives; thinly sliced
  • Potato chips; gently crushed

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs through a sieve to create an extra smooth mixture
  2. Transfer Boursin into a piping bag or ziploc bag, cut a 1/4 inch slit in corner of bag
  3. Heat a tablespoon of butter on medium low heat in a non stick skillet
  4. Add egg mixture to skillet, agitating immediately with a wooden spoon or rubber spatula
  5. Cook until top of omelette is mostly set
  6. Run spatula under the edges of the omelettle, and jiggle the pan to unstick it from the surface
  7. Remove from heat
  8. Pipe a generous line of Boursin down the centre of the omelette
  9. Holding the handle of the pan, carefully tilt it so the top of the omelette starts to curl onto itself
  10. Using the spatula curl the omelette over onto itself
  11. Similarly close the other side of the omelette unitl it is sealed over
  12. Gently transfer the omelette onto a plate, seam side down by inverting the pan, quickly but gently over the plate
  13. Rub the omelette with butter to add shine and provide adhesion to the toppings
  14. Garnish with thinly sliced chives and crushed potato chips
  15. Serve and Enjoy!
